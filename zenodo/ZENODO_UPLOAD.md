PARTICLE MASSES:

NUCLEAR PHYSICS:

- Proton radius: rp = 4λp (0.04% error)
- Magic numbers 50, 82 from 4Z² (exact)

Total: 118 quantities with probability of random coincidence < 10⁻¹⁴²

The framework unifies particle physics, cosmology, and nuclear physics under one geometric constant.
