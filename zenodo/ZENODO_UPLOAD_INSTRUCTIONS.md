## Step 7: Get Your DOI

After publishing, you'll receive a DOI like:
```
10.5281/zenodo.XXXXXXX
```

This is your permanent, citable reference!

---

## Step 8: Update Your Files

After receiving the DOI, update these files with the actual DOI:

1. **CITATION.cff** - Replace `XXXXXXX` with your DOI number
2. **README_ZENODO.md** - Update the citation block

Then create a **new version** on Zenodo with the updated files (this keeps the same base DOI).

## What the DOI Provides

1. **Timestamped Priority** - Proves when you published
2. **Permanent Record** - Cannot be deleted or altered
3. **Citable Reference** - Others can cite your work
4. **Discoverable** - Indexed by search engines and academic databases
5. **CERN Infrastructure** - Backed by CERN's reputation
