## Part 1: What Is M-Theory?

### The Five Superstring Theories

Before 1995, there were five consistent superstring theories:
1. Type I
2. Type IIA
3. Type IIB
4. Heterotic SO(32)
5. Heterotic E8 × E8

All in 10 dimensions.

Witten (1995) showed all five are limits of a single theory in **11 dimensions**: M-theory.

### Why 11?

**Nahm's theorem (1978):** Supergravity exists only in D ≤ 11.
- D = 11 is the maximum
- D = 10 allows superstrings
- D = 4 is our spacetime

**The dimensional hierarchy:**
```
11D: M-theory (fundamental)
 ↓
10D: Superstrings (compactified M-theory)
 ↓
4D:  Our spacetime (further compactification)
```
