**Project:** The Z² Framework / ANR-1 Algorithmic Neuro-Restoration
**Version:** 1.0
**Effective Date:** April 17, 2026

---

## Purpose

This Contributor License Agreement ("Agreement") establishes the terms under which contributions are made to the Z² Framework and ANR-1 projects ("the Project"). This Agreement is necessary to:

1. **Enable dual-licensing** — We must own the rights to offer both open-source and commercial licenses
2. **Protect contributors** — Clear ownership prevents future disputes
3. **Ensure the cure remains free** — Unified ownership guarantees humanitarian access forever

---

## Definitions

- **"Contribution"** means any source code, object code, documentation, hardware design, specification, algorithm, dataset, or other material submitted to the Project, including modifications, additions, or improvements to existing materials.

- **"Contributor"** means any individual or legal entity that submits a Contribution to the Project.

- **"Project Maintainer"** means Carl Zimmerman and designated successors or assigns.

- **"Submission"** means any form of communication sent to the Project, including Pull Requests, Issues, emails, or forum posts, containing a Contribution.

### 2. Joint Ownership

You agree that your Contribution shall be jointly owned by you and the Project Maintainer. This joint ownership means:

- Either party may independently license the Contribution
- The Project Maintainer may include the Contribution in commercial license agreements
- You retain the right to use your Contribution for any purpose

### 4. Representations and Warranties

You represent and warrant that:

a) **Original Work:** Your Contribution is your original creation, or you have sufficient rights to submit it under this Agreement.

b) **No Conflicts:** Your Contribution does not violate any agreement with a current or former employer, client, or other third party.

c) **No Encumbrances:** Your Contribution is not subject to any third-party intellectual property claims, licenses, or encumbrances that would restrict the Project's use.

d) **Legal Capacity:** You have the legal authority to enter into this Agreement. If you are submitting on behalf of an organization, you have authorization to bind that organization.

e) **Disclosure:** You will disclose any known intellectual property that may be relevant to your Contribution.

### 5. No Obligation

The Project Maintainer is under no obligation to:

- Accept or include your Contribution in the Project
- Provide compensation for your Contribution
- Credit you beyond standard attribution practices

### 6. Humanitarian Commitment

The Project Maintainer commits that:

a) **Free for Humanitarian Use:** The Project will always be available free of charge for academic research, humanitarian purposes, and open-science applications under appropriate open-source licenses.

b) **Commercial Licensing for Good:** Revenue from commercial licenses will be used to fund further research and support humanitarian deployment.

c) **Never Closed:** The core Project will never be converted to a fully proprietary, closed-source product.

## How to Accept This Agreement

### For Pull Request Contributors

By submitting a Pull Request to this repository, you acknowledge that:

1. You have read and understood this Contributor License Agreement
2. You agree to all terms contained herein
3. Your Contribution is submitted under these terms

Include the following statement in your Pull Request description:

```
I have read and agree to the Contributor License Agreement (CLA)
at CONTRIBUTING_CLA.md. I confirm that I have the right to submit
this contribution and that it may be used under the terms specified.
```

### For Significant Contributions

For contributions involving:
- Novel algorithms or methods
- Hardware designs
- Clinical protocols or datasets

Please also email [contact information] with:
- Your full legal name
- Affiliation (if applicable)
- Description of the Contribution
- Explicit statement agreeing to this CLA
