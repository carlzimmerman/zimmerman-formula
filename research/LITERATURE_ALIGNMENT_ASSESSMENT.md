1. **Absolute mass scale:** The framework doesn't explain why m_e = 0.511 MeV specifically.

2. **Individual fermion masses:** The full Yukawa matrix isn't derived from first principles.

3. **CP violation phases:** The CKM complex phase is not geometrically derived.

4. **Neutrino sector:** Neutrino masses and PMNS matrix not fully addressed.

5. **Λ_QCD exact formula:** The QCD scale prediction has 3% error (less precise than other results).

### What Could Conflict

1. **DESI w ≠ -1:** If dark energy equation of state differs from -1, the cosmological ratio derivation may need modification.

2. **BSM discoveries:** Any new particles would require framework extension.
