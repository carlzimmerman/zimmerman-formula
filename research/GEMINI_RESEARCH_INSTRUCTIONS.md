## Research Tasks for Gemini

### Task 1: Find Additional Cosmological Tests

Search for:
- **BAO measurements** from DESI, eBOSS, BOSS that constrain Ω_m, Ω_Λ
- **CMB experiments** beyond Planck (ACT, SPT, BICEP, CLASS)
- **Supernova surveys** (Pantheon+, DES, LSST) measuring w
- **Weak lensing** results constraining dark energy
- **21cm cosmology** experiments (HERA, SKA precursors)

### Task 2: Find Particle Physics Tests

Search for:
- **Precision electroweak** measurements at LHC
- **Higgs coupling** measurements that test λ_H predictions
- **EDM experiments** (electron, neutron, atoms)
- **Lepton universality** tests at LHCb and Belle II
- **Rare decay** measurements (B_s → μμ, K → πνν)

### Task 3: Find Modified Gravity Tests

Search for:
- **Lunar laser ranging** constraints on gravity
- **Pulsar timing arrays** (NANOGrav, EPTA, PPTA) gravitational wave background
- **Galaxy cluster dynamics** beyond El Gordo
- **Dwarf galaxy kinematics** testing MOND predictions
- **Strong gravitational lensing** time delays

### Task 4: Find Quantum Gravity/Lorentz Tests

Search for:
- **Lorentz invariance** tests from gamma-ray bursts
- **Photon dispersion** tests from distant sources
- **Equivalence principle** tests (MICROSCOPE, STEP)
- **Atomic clock** comparisons testing relativity

### Task 5: Find Unexplained Values

Search for:
- **Cosmological coincidences** that lack explanation
- **Fine-tuning problems** in particle physics
- **Anomalies** in experiments (any >3σ deviations from SM)
- **Unexplained ratios** or relationships between constants

## What Z² Framework DOES NOT Predict (Yet)

These areas have gaps in the Z² framework:
- Hierarchy problem (17 orders between M_EW and M_Pl)
- Absolute neutrino masses
- Quark and lepton mass spectrum details
- Strong CP problem (θ_QCD value)

Finding experimental constraints on these may help develop the framework further.
