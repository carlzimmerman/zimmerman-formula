### 7. How Complex Structure Might Emerge

**Carl's conjecture:**

The cube has 3 mutually perpendicular face-pairs. Each pair defines a plane with 4-fold rotational symmetry. A 90° rotation $R$ satisfies:

$$R^2 = 180° = -I$$

This is the defining property of $\sqrt{-1}$. Three perpendicular rotations give quaternion structure:

$$i^2 = j^2 = k^2 = ijk = -1$$

Complex numbers arise as any single quaternion imaginary axis.

**Status:** Suggestive but not rigorously proven.
