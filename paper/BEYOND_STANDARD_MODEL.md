SUSY was expected at M_SUSY ~ 1 TeV to solve the hierarchy problem.

### What Z Says

The hierarchy is solved by:
```
M_Pl = 2v × Z²¹·⁵
```

No SUSY needed! The hierarchy is geometric, not requiring cancellation.

### If SUSY Exists Anyway

The SUSY scale would be:
```
M_SUSY = M_Pl / Z^k
```

For k = 21: M_SUSY ~ 1 TeV (excluded by LHC)
For k = 20: M_SUSY ~ 6 TeV (marginally allowed)
For k = 19: M_SUSY ~ 35 TeV (safe)

### Prediction

```
M_SUSY > M_Pl / Z¹⁹ ~ 35 TeV

Or: SUSY doesn't exist
```

The framework prefers NO low-energy SUSY.

No large or warped extra dimensions at accessible scales.

String effects appear at the GUT scale.
